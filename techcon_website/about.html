History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local event for developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences, attracting thousands of attendees from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to inspire innovation, promote collaboration, and provide a platform for sharing groundbreaking technological advancements. Our goal is to empower the next generation of tech leaders and foster inclusive growth across all areas of the industry.
